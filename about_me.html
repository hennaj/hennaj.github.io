About Me

Welcome! I'm Henna and I'm currently in beautiful Colorado finishing up my M.S. in BioAstro. Sounds cool? Because it is ;) Through my program, I'm working on becoming a better Spacecraft Life Support Engineer. I graduated from MIT in June 2014 with my B.S. in AeroAstro with Information Technology. Through MIT, I had oppotunities to work in multiple labs: Man Vehicle Lab and Nano-Engineered Composite Structures, take some cool classes, and make incredible friends. Challenges that get me excited include space suit design, big data, data processing, spacecraft sensor suites, and machine learning for drones. Through projects and research, I've been able to get experience in these interests.
