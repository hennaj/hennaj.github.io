Hi. Welcome to my portfolio website.

I graduated from MIT with my B.S. in AeroAstro with Information Technology. I am currently an M.S. candidate at CU Boulder in BioAstro: Spacecraft Life Support Engineering. Below is a slideshow of the projects and research I've worked on over the last few years.

Martian Greenhouse Prototype Sensors

Lead electrical and software engineer for CU Boulder's team for the NASA Sponsored eXploration Habitat (X-Hab) competition In the span of nine months, I led the design and development of the electrical and software components necessary to implement autonomous plant growth and maintenance in an enclosed reduced-scale greenhouse.

Artificial Gravity Test Vehicle

Developed an Artificial Gravity test vehicle with three other team members. Worked on all aspects of the vehicle from preliminary dynamics analysis, to mechanical construction and electrical integration. With my team, I conducted data collection on a microgravity parabolic maneuver flight via Zero G Corp.

Embedded Systems: Strobe Water Lab

I proposed, designed and built a microcontroller - based lab containment to illustrate the visual illusion of levitating water droplets by directing strobing LEDs at oscillating streams of water. I worked individually on the planning, designing, building, coding and wiring of this project.

Multifunctional SuperCapacitor

For our senior year thesis, my project partner and I designed and fabricated samples of a multifunctional supercapacitor. We investigated whether energy could be stored on the Carbon Nanotubes(CNTs) incorpororated into a layup. I worked on the design, CNT growth in the lab, composite layups, supercapacitor assembly, and testing.

JPL BioSleeve using Machine Learning

Wrote support vector machine code for the JPL BioSleeve - a sleeve of EMG electrodes used as a control paradigm for prosthetic and hands-free robotic control. Collaborated with two other engineering students and two JPL Engineers. Worked in MATLAB and Java. Worked on improving classification algorithms and sleeve design.

Electric Propulsion Analysis

Documented MATLAB analysis of Electric propulsion and chemical rockets on a website that can be reached by clicking the above heading. Wrote MATLAB integrators and optimization code using Edelbaum and Hohmann equations.
